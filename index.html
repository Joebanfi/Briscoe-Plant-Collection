About Me

I am a designer and illustrator based in South West London, UK.

My work is primarily based upon houseplants found within our small London flat. I started drawing in my spare time, and work as a Landscape Architect + Garden designer in London. I have a love for the natural environment and nature which influences my work. Each piece is hand drawn in pen, and brought to life through the introduction of colour which brings vibrancy to each illustration.

Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy!
